## Core Love Understanding

### Love Nature

#### Foundational Principles

- **Central Human Capacity**: Love as fundamental human capacity and driving force
- **Action Origin**: Love as root source of all human actions and behavior
- **Ultimate Purpose**: Divine love as ultimate human purpose and fulfillment
- **Spiritual Essence**: Love as essence of spiritual journey and worship
- **Transformative Power**: Love as profound transformative force for heart and character

#### Love Definition

- **Essence Components**:

  - Heart inclination toward the beloved (ميل القلب)
  - Pleasure in proximity and presence (التلذذ بالقرب)
  - Preference above all else (الإيثار على ما سواه)
  - Compliance with beloved's wishes (موافقة المحبوب)
  - Heart attachment and preoccupation (تعلق القلب)

- **Manifestation Elements**:

  - Seeking beloved's pleasure (طلب رضا المحبوب)
  - Prioritizing beloved's wishes (تقديم مراد المحبوب)
  - Continuous remembrance (دوام الذكر)
  - Longing for meeting (الشوق إلى اللقاء)
  - Discomfort in separation (التألم بالبعد)

- **Complete Definition Integration**:
  - Perfect heart inclination toward the beloved
  - Complete preference over everything else
  - Full compliance with beloved's wishes
  - Constant preoccupation and remembrance
  - Comprehensive life orientation around beloved

#### Love Categories

- **Primary Classification**:

  - Natural love (محبة طبيعية) - innate inclinations
  - Divine love (محبة إلهية) - love for Allah
  - Love for Allah's sake (الحب في الله) - loving what Allah loves
  - Love with Allah (الحب مع الله) - polytheistic love
  - Worldly love (محبة دنيوية) - love of temporal things

- **Love Relationship Types**:

  - Love for Allah (محبة لله)
  - Love for what Allah loves (محبة لما يحب الله)
  - Love for the sake of Allah (محبة في الله)
  - Love that is with Allah (محبة مع الله)

- **Divine Love Relations**:
  - Allah's love for servants (محبة الله لعباده)
  - Servants' love for Allah (محبة العباد لله)
  - Mutual love relationship (المحبة المتبادلة)
  - Love manifestation consequences (آثار المحبة)
